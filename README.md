## What This Is

Star Wars is 47+ years of movies, animated series, and live-action shows across multiple timelines. Watching it for the first time without a roadmap is genuinely confusing.

This guide solves that. It covers:

- **Every movie, show, and animated series** worth watching
- **The recommended watch order** (Modified Machete Order — preserves the big twist)
- **Fan edits** for films that benefit from them, with per-edit pros/cons
- **Community negatives** pulled honestly from Reddit, fanedit.org, Steam forums, and elsewhere — no sugarcoating
- **What to skip** and why

## How to Find Fan Edits

Fan edits are **not commercially available** — they are distributed as patch files (`.xdelta` format) that are applied to an official Blu-ray disc rip.

**Steps:**
1. Own the official Blu-ray (required — the patch does not include the film itself)
2. Rip your disc to a compatible format
3. Download the `.xdelta` patch file from the community
4. Apply the patch using [xdelta3](https://github.com/jmacd/xdelta)

**Where to find them:**
- [fanedit.org](https://fanedit.org) — largest fan edit community, step-by-step guides available
- [r/fanedits](https://reddit.com/r/fanedits) — community discussion, links, and recommendations
- Searching `[Editor Name] fanedit` will usually surface the right threads

## What to Skip

| Title | Reason |
|-------|--------|
| The Phantom Menace *(theatrical)* | Jar Jar, midichlorians, weak pacing — use the fan edit or skip per Machete Order |
| The Clone Wars 2008 Movie | Rated poorly — it's a pilot stitched together as a film |
| Clone Wars Seasons 1–2 filler | Childish tone, low ratings — jump to curated arcs |
| Book of Boba Fett *(most of it)* | Only Episodes 5–6 matter for Mandalorian continuity |
| The Acolyte | Cancelled after Season 1 with no resolution |
| Solo *(first watch)* | Non-essential — save for after your first full saga run |
